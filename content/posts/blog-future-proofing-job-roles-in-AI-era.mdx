In November of 2025, I had the unique opportunity to represent students at the [GovAI Summit](https://events.govtech.com/GovAI-Coalition-Summit) 2025.
I participated as a panel speaker discussing the measures taken to ensure job readiness among students, and the short- and long-term impact of generative AI on job roles.

# Background

About two months prior to the event, I was invited by Dr. Yingjie Liu to participate as a speaker on a panel discussing the impact of AI on job responsibilities.
As a student preparing to enter the job market, my perspective on job readiness, upskilling in emerging technology, and my experience interacting with students across various backgrounds could offer a unique angle to the concerns associated with AI use.

I'd like to sincerely thank the organizers and the staff for organizing a smooth event.
They even let me to keep my tent card as a memento!
